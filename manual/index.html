Privacy Policy for Tap Talk Visuals

Last Updated: 2/12/2025

Tap Talk Visuals (“the App”) is designed for young children and strictly adheres to privacy and safety regulations. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data

The App does not require account creation, sign-in, or any personal input.

How the App Works

  • All features work fully offline on your device
  • No data is sent to any server

Children’s Privacy

Tap Talk Visuals complies with the Google Play Families Policy and the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Since no data is collected, no child’s personal information is stored, shared, or processed.

Permissions

The App does not request any sensitive permissions.

It only uses basic audio playback to pronounce words within the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if new features are added. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.
